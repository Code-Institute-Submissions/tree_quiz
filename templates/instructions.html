How to Play

  • You will be presented with 10 pictures of common Irish trees.
  • You will get two attempts to enter the name of the tree.
  • 10 points for correct answer on first attempt.
  • 5 points for correct answer on first attempt.
  • Your game play is saved, linked to your username. So choose a unique name?
  • You can exit the game at any stage by clicking the Home or Leaderboard menu options, or by closing the tab.
  • You can take up where you left off at any stage by simply signing in with your username.
  • Play as many times as you like and try to make it onto the top 5 leaderboard.
  • Your score will only be added to the leaderboard if its your new personnel best.
